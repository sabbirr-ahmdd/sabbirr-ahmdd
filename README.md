## LeadLeap

LeadLeap is the first product built by Orviton.

Every day, businesses lose potential customers because visitors
leave a website before submitting a contact form. These
abandoned leads often disappear without a trace.

LeadLeap solves this with real-time lead capture and automated
lead recovery.

Instead of waiting for a form submission, LeadLeap captures
visitor information as soon as a visitor enters their name and
email into a website form. If the visitor leaves before
submitting the form, an automated follow-up email is sent
instantly to help businesses reconnect with that potential
customer.

By recovering abandoned leads, LeadLeap helps businesses
generate more opportunities, improve conversions, and close
more deals — without changing their existing website.
